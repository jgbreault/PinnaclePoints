A pinnacle point is a point from which no higher point can be seen. After defining pinnacle points in 2023, I wrote an algorithm to find all pinnacle points on Earth with over 300 m of prominence or over 160 km of isolation. I also built an interactive map of the 885 identified points. Here, I record my summitted pinnacle points, with tips for summitting them yourself.
